MO:MO

Momos are a popular dumpling from Tibet, Nepal, and parts of India. They start with a dough made from all-purpose flour and water, rolled into thin circles.
The filling typically consists of ground meat or finely chopped vegetables mixed with spices like ginger, garlic, and soy sauce.
A spoonful of this mixture is placed on each dough circle.
The dough is then folded and sealed around the filling into pleated pouches or half-moon shapes. Momos are usually steamed, though they can also be boiled or pan-fried.
Served with a spicy dipping sauce, they are a savory and aromatic treat enjoyed worldwide.

Ingredients:

  • ground meat
  • flour
  • Spices : salt, pepper, cumin, etc
  • onion
  • cilantro
  • utensils to cook it

steps :

  • First knead the dough nicely and put it aside.
  • Then mix the ground meat with spices and onions , cilantro etc
  • Then wrap the meat inside the dough .
  • Then steam it for about 20 mins.
