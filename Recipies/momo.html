Odin Recipie for MO:MO

Ingredients

flour, ground meat (any), salt, pepper, cumin, garlic powder, garlic, onion, coriander Prep Time: 20min Cook time: 30min Total time: 40min
  1. First knead the dough until smooth and let it rest.
  2. Then mix the ground meat with the spices and diced onions with garlic thoroughy.
  3. Then get some small sized circular dough and fill it and close it with the mixed ground meat.
  4. Then use a pot with boiling water and steam them for about 30 minutes.
  5. Your, MO:MO's are ready
